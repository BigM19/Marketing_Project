## 2. Competitor Analysis

*   **Strengths:** Strong brand recognition, integration with the Microsoft ecosystem, and a wide range of features. (Source: Website content analysis of Microsoft, search results on marketing strategies).
*   **Weaknesses:** Pricing may be a barrier for some SMEs, and the tool might be part of a larger suite, which may not be ideal for all users. (Source: Pricing information, and general market analysis).

*   **Strengths:** User-friendly interface, ability to analyze data from various sources, and strong focus on data visualization. (Source: Website content analysis of Julius AI).

### 2.3 Excelmatic

*   **Strengths:** AI-powered features for data cleaning, chart generation, and report creation. (Source: Website content analysis of Excelmatic).
